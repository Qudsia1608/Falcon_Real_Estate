About Falcon Real Estate

Founded in 1993 under the ownership of Mr. Mohamed Safdar, Falcon Real Estate embarked on its journey as a real estate consultancy firm, specializing in meeting the residential and commercial space requisites of corporate clients. Over the years, we have evolved into a trusted entity, serving a diverse clientele including leading multinational corporations, international banks, High Net Worth Individuals (HNIs), and Non-Resident Indians (NRIs).

Expanding our scope, we ventured into investment opportunities and exclusive marketing collaborations with developers. Notably, we have successfully executed sole marketing endeavors for prestigious projects such as Cunningham Classic on Cunningham Road, Regent Classic on Indiranagar 100ft road, Regent Crown on Koramangala 80 ft road, Regent Point in Cambridge layout, Regent Sunnyside on Koramangala 80 ft road, Regent Insignia on Koramangala 100 ft road, Regent Gateway in Whitefield (EPIP), Regent Cornette in Koramangala, Regent Prime on Whitefield main road, Ali Plaza in Kammanhalli and others.

Drawing from our extensive experience and expertise in the property market, we have facilitated the acquisition of commercial plots for our investors, meticulously conducting due diligence on titles and providing comprehensive feasibility reports from architects.

Our commitment extends beyond mere transactions; we are dedicated to guiding our investors in making informed decisions regarding commercial property acquisitions, assisting them in leasing out properties to generate substantial monthly rental income, and offering comprehensive property management solutions. Moreover, we actively engage in funding construction projects, overseeing project management endeavors, and connecting our clients with suitable tenants across diverse industries including hospitality, dining establishments, furniture, IT enterprises, jewelry, and plug-and-play facilities, wherever synergiesÂ arise.
